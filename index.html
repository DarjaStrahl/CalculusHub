About us

Calculus Hub is a non-profit organization. We are based in Hamburg. Our dedicated and enthusiastic team set up entertaining events on a regular basis in Hamburg.

Local

We connect math enthusiasts in and around Hamburg. We organise events to meet people who are working in Hamburg.

Community

Mathematics
